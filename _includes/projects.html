  • Frontend: ReactJS

This site helps you to store photos of your friends/family in an organized manner. You make seperate profiles of each person and just tag one or more than one of them on the photos you upload. On their birthday/anniversary or any special day, you get a reminder and a collage/video made. Plus you can access all of these photos in one page so that you can post them on other social media websites like Facebook.
